Duration

Minimum 8 weeks — flexible according to academic requirements and organizational needs.

Location

PODA Head Office, Islamabad. Opportunities may also be available at project locations, subject to organizational requirements.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Students currently enrolled in Bachelor's or Master's degree programs, or fresh graduates.
  • Strong communication, analytical, and interpersonal skills.
  • Interest in social development, women's empowerment, and the NGO sector.
  • Proficiency in MS Office and internet research will be an added advantage.

  • Practical experience in a leading development organization.
  • Exposure to project implementation and community engagement.
  • Professional mentoring and learning opportunities.
  • Development of research, reporting, administrative, and organizational skills.
  • Internship Certificate upon successful completion of the program.

Become a Volunteer

  • Flexible commitment — help out at a single event, campaign or on an ongoing basis.

By Invitation Honorary

Honorary Membership

Reserved for individuals who have made an outstanding, sustained contribution to PODA's mission or to rural women's rights in Pakistan more broadly. Honorary members are nominated by the Board of Directors.

No membership fee Board nomination only
Nominate a Member

Member Benefits

  • Voting rights at PODA's Annual General Meeting (individual and organizational members).
  • Priority invitations to the Annual Rural Women Leadership Training Conference and member-only briefings.
  • Early access to PODA's Annual Report, research studies and publications.
  • A direct, ongoing role in shaping PODA's advocacy priorities and program direction.
Eligibility

Open to individuals aged 18 and above who support PODA's mission, and to registered organizations aligned with women's rights, rural development or human rights work in Pakistan. Membership is subject to Board approval.

How to Apply

Submit the following to apply for membership.

1

Membership Application Form — request the form using the button below and return it completed.

2

CNIC copy (individual applicants) or registration certificate (organizational applicants).

3

A short note on why you'd like to join and how you'd like to contribute.

4

Membership fee, payable on approval by the Board.

Note: Send your completed application to info@poda.org.pk with the subject line "Membership Application — [Your Name / Organization]". Applications are reviewed at the next Board meeting.
