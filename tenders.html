Our Procurement Principles

  • Fair & transparent — all tenders are open to qualified vendors and evaluated against published criteria.
  • Competitive — procurement above donor thresholds requires a minimum number of competing bids.
  • Compliant — every process follows the procurement policies of the relevant donor (EU, NED, UN agencies and others).
  • Accountable — procurement decisions are documented and available to donors and auditors on request.
Procurement Queries

For questions about an open tender, contact our procurement team at info@poda.org.pk quoting the tender reference number.

How to Submit a Bid

Follow these steps to submit a competitive bid for an open tender.

1

Request the tender document — email us quoting the reference number to receive the full RFQ / ToR and bid submission format.

2

Prepare your technical & financial proposal in line with the submission format provided.

3

Include company / consultant registration documents (tax registration, relevant licenses, CVs for consultancy bids).

4

Submit before the deadline — late bids are not accepted under PODA's donor-compliant procurement policy.

Note: Submit bids to info@poda.org.pk with the subject line "Bid Submission — [Tender Reference Number]". Only complete bids received before the deadline will be evaluated.
