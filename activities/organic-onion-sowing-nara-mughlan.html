Organic Onion Sowing at PODA Organic Nara Mughlan

20 January 2026 Climate Smart Agriculture & Green Job Skills

On 20 January 2026, PODA's Sustainable Agriculture and Climate Change Farm Team carried out the sowing of organic onion seedlings at the PODA organic farm in Nara Mughlan.

The team prepared the planting beds by hand and transplanted the seedlings with careful attention to spacing and soil conditions, laying the groundwork for a healthy crop. The activity falls under Climate Smart Agriculture & Green Job Skills, which trains rural youth in hands-on, climate-resilient farming practice at PODA's own demonstration farm.
