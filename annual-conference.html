PODA's Flagship Gathering

Annual Rural Women Leadership Training Conference

PODA's flagship gathering of rural women leaders from across Pakistan, now in its 18th year — producing policy resolutions on climate justice, civic participation and legal identity rights.

Overview

A Resolution-Driven Conference, Not Just an Event

Held every year in Islamabad on or around 15 October — the UN International Day of Rural Women — the Annual Rural Women Leadership Training Conference brings together rural women leaders, policymakers and civil society to develop practical resolutions on the issues affecting their communities. Running since 2008, it has grown into PODA's flagship national platform for transforming rural women from vulnerable populations into effective change makers, building the partnerships and linkages that carry those resolutions into policy.

The 18th edition, held 15–17 October 2025, drew more than 1,500 rural women leaders from 100 districts to shape recommendations on climate justice, civic participation and legal identity rights.
