Beyond Barriers: Promoting Capacity Building, Disability Inclusion, Safeguarding, and Gender Equity for Pakistani Women With and Without Disabilities

Thematic Area

Disability Inclusion, Women's Economic Empowerment, Gender Equality, GBVH, Safeguarding, Inclusive Employment, Policy Advocacy

Duration

April 2026 – March 2029

Donor Agency

European Union (EU)

Location

Punjab & Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (Peshawar, Mardan, Chakwal, Multan)

Key Interventions

  • Strengthens the institutional capacity of women-led organizations and Organizations of Women with Disabilities (OWDs) through organizational assessments, capacity-building programs, mentoring, training, and institutional development support.
  • Promotes economic inclusion by conducting accessibility assessments of Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) institutions, facilitating market-oriented skills development, disability confidence training, and employment linkages for women with and without disabilities.
  • Engages employers, Business Development Service providers, and TVET institutions to adopt disability-inclusive policies, implement reasonable accommodation measures, and promote accessible and inclusive workplace practices.
  • Conducts participatory research on Gender-Based Violence and Harassment (GBVH), develops disability-inclusive referral pathways, and strengthens evidence-based advocacy to foster safer, more inclusive, and equitable workplaces.
  • Facilitates policy dialogue with government departments, labor market stakeholders, and relevant institutions to strengthen implementation of the SME Policy 2021, the Protection Against Harassment of Women at the Workplace Act 2022, and the National Action Plan on Business and Human Rights.
Ongoing

Promoting Civic Leadership and Participation

Thematic Area

Democratic Governance, Women's Leadership, Civic Participation, Legal Empowerment, Digital Safety, Human Rights, Community Mobilization

Duration

February 2026 – January 2027

Donor Agency

National Endowment for Democracy (NED)

Location

Pakistan — Punjab, Islamabad Capital Territory, and participants from 100+ districts nationwide

Key Interventions

  • Strengthens the Rural Women's Network through leadership development, governance, and civic engagement training to enhance democratic participation and women's leadership.
  • Expands access to legal identity and justice by providing mobile legal aid clinics, civil registration support, and free legal aid in collaboration with district bar associations and pro bono lawyers.
  • Conducts training on digital safety, democratic values, constitutional rights, civic education, and media literacy to strengthen women's and youth's digital resilience and informed civic participation.
  • Organizes weekly webinars, public awareness campaigns, and radio programs to promote women's rights, legal identity, electoral participation, democratic engagement, and civic awareness.
  • Convenes the Annual Rural Women Leadership Conference and implements organizational development initiatives to strengthen institutional sustainability, strategic planning, governance systems, and staff capacity.
